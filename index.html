About Me

I am Chu Doan Phuong Chi, a student of Class 11 English 2 at Hanoi – Amsterdam High School for the Gifted. I have a strong academic record with an SAT score of 1560/1600 (perfect 800 in Math, top 99%) and IELTS Academic 8.0 (Listening 9.0, Reading 9.0), and I won a prize at the Hanoi City English Contest in grade 9. I love organizing events and community work: I served on the Finance and Logistics teams of projects such as the English Performance musical, the Humanoid photography project and The 1% Fund fundraiser.
